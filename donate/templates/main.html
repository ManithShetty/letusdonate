What is Letusdonate?

Letusdonate is a Non-Profit network that facilitates interaction between donors and NGO's from different parts of the country. It is place where donors can make donation to variety of charity and for the good cause,aslo NGO's can reach out to people for organizing charity events and also get in contact with volunteers.

How it works

When donors make donation in the website,volunteer collects the items from donors and distributes to the NGO's as per the NGO requirements.The donors can also volunteer for any charity events,if he wants to volunteer to collect items he can request for the same.
